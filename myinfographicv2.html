Introduction & Purpose

This presentation provides an exhaustive summary of findings from the DANA-014 Exit Survey, encompassing all program components, quantitative metrics, and detailed qualitative feedback. Our objective is to gain a granular, holistic understanding of learner satisfaction, identify specific program strengths, pinpoint precise areas for improvement with supporting evidence, and inform strategic, data-driven enhancements for future cohorts.

Overall Program Performance: Net Promoter Score (NPS)

A very strong NPS of 77.3 indicates exceptional overall learner satisfaction and a significant willingness to recommend the program. The remarkably low percentage of detractors suggests that negative experiences are rare and contained.

Key Recommendations: The Path Forward

Based on comprehensive learner feedback, we propose six core recommendations to significantly enhance the program experience for future cohorts.

1

Adjust Program Pacing & Duration for Deeper Learning

Extend time for complex topics (SQL, Python, portfolio projects). Offer optional review sessions and more practice assignments to reinforce learning.

2

Enhance Live Session Accessibility & Promote Asynchronous Options

Introduce a wider range of live class times (e.g., early morning, late evening, weekend slots) to better accommodate diverse work shifts and time zones. Actively promote and clarify the benefits of asynchronous lecture attendance via recorded sessions.

3

Improve Proactive Communication & Personalized Support

Increase the frequency and proactivity of check-ins from CSCs and all staff. Ensure timely responses and provide clear, consistent guidance on deadlines and expectations.

4

Provide Targeted Training for Instructors & TAs

Implement additional training for TAs and instructors on pacing, clarity, and communication techniques, especially for those with language barriers. Standardize teaching methods and ensure clear, step-by-step explanations for complex topics.

5

Refine Professional Development for Targeted Impact

Develop more targeted, individualized support for career development and ensure materials are continuously updated. Integrate more interactive elements like mock interviews.

6

Optimize Technology Tools for Seamless Experience

Prioritize addressing technical issues with platforms like Codio. Manage Slack channels for better focus. Consider extending access duration to learning resources post-program.
